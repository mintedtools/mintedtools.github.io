meaningslong: "The HC error indicates that the washer has detected abnormally high water temperature entering the drum. Samsung specifies that this occurs when the hot water supply is too hot, when the cold and hot hoses are reversed, or when the heating system malfunctions. Excessive water temperature can damage fabrics, disrupt wash cycles, and trigger safety shutdowns. The washer monitors inlet temperature and internal heating, and if the temperature exceeds safe limits, it displays the HC code."

  - "Temperature settings do not match actual water temperature"
causes:
  - "Hot and cold hoses reversed"
  - "Water heater temperature set too high"
  - "Faulty inlet valve allowing hot water into cold cycles"
  - "Heating element malfunction"
  - "Residual hot water in plumbing lines"
stepfixes:
  - "Verify that the hot and cold hoses are connected correctly."
