code: "LC"
meaningshort: "Washer detects a drain hose issue."
meaninglong: "The LC error indicates that the washer has detected a problem with the drain hose position or water flow. Samsung specifies that this code appears when the end of the drain hose is placed incorrectly, when the hose is clogged, or when water cannot exit the washer at the expected rate. The washer monitors backflow and drainage resistance, and if the system senses improper hose placement or blockage, it triggers the LC code to prevent flooding or siphoning."
symptoms:
  - "Washer stops mid‑cycle and displays LC"
  - "Water drains slowly or not at all"
  - "Washer repeatedly attempts to drain"
  - "Gurgling or bubbling sounds from the drain hose"
  - "Water backs up into the drum after draining"
causes:
  - "Drain hose placed directly on the floor"
  - "Drain hose inserted too deeply into the standpipe"
  - "Clogged or partially blocked drain hose"
  - "Household drain system restricted or slow"
  - "Improper drain height causing siphoning"
stepfixes:
  - "Ensure the drain hose is elevated to the correct height."
  - "Remove the hose from the floor and secure it properly."
  - "Inspect the hose for clogs, bends, or crushing."
  - "Clear any blockages in the household drain system."
  - "Restart the washer after correcting hose placement."
safetynotes:
  - "Unplug the washer before adjusting the drain hose."
  - "Do not run the washer with the hose lying flat on the floor."
  - "Avoid forcing the hose into tight drain openings."
