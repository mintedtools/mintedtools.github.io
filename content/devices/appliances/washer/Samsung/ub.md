meaningshort: "Unbalanced load detected during spin."
meaninglong: "The Ub error indicates that the washer has detected an unbalanced load during the spin cycle. Samsung explains that this occurs when laundry is unevenly distributed, when a single heavy item is washed alone, or when the washer is not level. The washer attempts to rebalance the load automatically, but if it cannot achieve stable rotation, it stops spinning to prevent excessive vibration and mechanical damage."
symptoms:
  - "Washer shakes or vibrates excessively"
  - "Spin cycle does not complete"
  - "Washer repeatedly tries to rebalance the load"
  - "Washer stops mid‑spin with Ub displayed"
  - "Clothes remain wet at the end of the cycle"
causes:
  - "Single heavy item causing imbalance"
  - "Mixed load types (e.g., towels with light fabrics)"
  - "Washer not level on the floor"
  - "Laundry tangled into a ball"
  - "Shock absorbers worn or weakened"
stepfixes:
  - "Redistribute the laundry evenly inside the drum."
  - "Add one or two additional items when washing a single heavy garment."
  - "Ensure the washer is level using the adjustable feet."
  - "Untangle twisted or balled‑up laundry."
  - "Restart the spin cycle after correcting the imbalance."
safetynotes:
  - "Do not continue running the washer if it vibrates violently."
  - "Avoid washing single heavy items alone."
  - "Unplug the washer before adjusting leveling feet."
