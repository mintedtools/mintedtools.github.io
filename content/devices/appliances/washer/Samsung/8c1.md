meaninglong: "The 8C1 error indicates that the washer has detected a malfunction in the MEMS (Micro‑Electro‑Mechanical System) vibration sensor. This sensor monitors drum balance, vibration levels, and spin stability. Samsung specifies that 8C1 is a variant of the 8C family of MEMS sensor errors, triggered when the sensor sends irregular data, loses communication with the control board, or fails internal diagnostics. The washer halts high‑speed spin to prevent excessive vibration, noise, or mechanical damage."

  - "Faulty MEMS vibration sensor"

  - "Severe unbalance triggering sensor protection"
