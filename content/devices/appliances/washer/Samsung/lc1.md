meaningshort: "Washer detects a drain hose blockage or improper placement."
meaninglong: "The LC1 error is a variant of the LC code and indicates that the washer has detected a more severe or persistent drain hose issue. Samsung notes that this occurs when the drain hose is clogged, incorrectly positioned, or unable to move water out of the washer. LC1 often appears when the hose is placed too low, inserted too far into the drain pipe, or when the drain system is obstructed. The washer halts the cycle to prevent backflow and potential flooding."
symptoms:
  - "Washer stops and displays LC1"
  - "Water remains in the drum after draining"
  - "Washer repeatedly attempts to pump water out"
  - "Drain hose vibrates or pulses during operation"
  - "Water backs up into the drum after draining"
causes:
  - "Drain hose clogged with lint or debris"
  - "Drain hose inserted too deeply into the standpipe"
  - "Drain hose lying flat on the floor"
  - "Household drain system blocked"
  - "Improper drain height causing siphoning"
stepfixes:
  - "Remove and inspect the drain hose for blockages."
  - "Ensure the hose is elevated to the correct height."
  - "Pull the hose back so it is not inserted too far into the drain."
  - "Clear any obstructions in the household drain system."
  - "Restart the washer after correcting the hose position."
safetynotes:
  - "Unplug the washer before removing or adjusting the drain hose."
  - "Keep a towel or tray ready when disconnecting the hose."
  - "Do not operate the washer until proper drainage is restored."
