code: "dC"
meaningshort: "Door is not properly closed."
meaninglong: "The dC error indicates that the washer detects an open or improperly latched door. Samsung explains that the washer will not tumble or spin unless the door lock mechanism fully engages. Laundry caught in the door, a weak latch, or a misaligned door strike can prevent proper locking. The washer performs a door‑lock check before filling, and if the lock does not engage, it triggers the dC code to prevent leaks and safety hazards."
symptoms:
  - "Washer will not start the cycle"
  - "Clicking sounds as the washer attempts to lock the door"
  - "Cycle pauses immediately after pressing Start"
  - "Door lock light does not illuminate"
  - "Washer drains briefly but does not fill"
causes:
  - "Door not fully closed"
  - "Laundry trapped between the door and diaphragm"
  - "Faulty or obstructed door lock mechanism"
  - "Misaligned door hinge or strike plate"
  - "Child Lock interfering with operation"
stepfixes:
  - "Open the door and remove any trapped laundry."
  - "Firmly close the door until the latch clicks."
  - "Inspect the rubber diaphragm for obstructions."
  - "Check that the door hinge is aligned and not sagging."
  - "Disable Child Lock if active."
  - "Restart the washer after confirming proper closure."
safetynotes:
  - "Do not force the door closed if resistance is felt; check for obstructions."
  - "Avoid slamming the door, which may damage the latch."
  - "Unplug the washer before inspecting the door lock assembly."
