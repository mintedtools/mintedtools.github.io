symptoms:
  - "Drum does not fill"
  - "Cycle pauses early"
  - "Repeated clicking from inlet valves"
  - "Washer drains for 3 minutes and disables the power button"
  - "Long fill times or no water entering"
causes:
  - "Closed or partially opened water taps"
  - "Frozen inlet hoses"
  - "Clogged mesh filter"
  - "Kinked or crushed hoses"
  - "Low household water pressure"
  - "Hot/cold hoses reversed"
stepfixes:
  - "Fully open both water taps."
  - "Inspect hoses for kinks, bends, or crushing."
  - "Disconnect hoses and clean the mesh filters."
  - "Thaw frozen hoses slowly."
  - "Ensure cold and hot taps are correctly connected."
  - "Restart the washer after restoring water flow."
safetynotes:
  - "Unplug the washer before disconnecting hoses."
  - "Do not force frozen hoses; thaw gradually."
  - "Avoid running the washer with insufficient water pressure."
