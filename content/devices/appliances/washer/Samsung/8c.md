meaninglong: "The 8C error indicates that the washer's MEMS (Micro‑Electro‑Mechanical System) vibration sensor is not working properly. Samsung explains that this sensor monitors drum balance, vibration levels, and spin stability. If the sensor fails, sends irregular data, or loses communication with the control board, the washer cannot safely perform high‑speed spins. The system halts operation to prevent excessive vibration, noise, or mechanical damage."
