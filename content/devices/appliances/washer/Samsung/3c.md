meaninglong: "The 3C error indicates that the washer has detected a problem with the motor or its ability to rotate the drum. Samsung specifies that this code appears when the motor fails to start, rotates irregularly, or encounters excessive resistance. This may occur due to an overloaded drum, wiring issues, or a failing motor control board. The washer halts the cycle to prevent overheating or mechanical damage."

  - "Cycle restarts but fails again during spin"

  - "Worn motor components"
