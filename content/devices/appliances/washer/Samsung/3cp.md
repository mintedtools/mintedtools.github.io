meaninglong: "The 3CP error indicates that the washer has detected an internal electronic control issue. Samsung specifies that this code appears when the control system cannot properly manage motor operation, cycle timing, or internal communication. This may occur due to temporary software faults, unstable power, or a failing control board. The washer halts operation to prevent damage to the motor, inverter, or other electronic components."

  - "Washer restarts unexpectedly"
  - "Buttons respond slowly or intermittently"
causes:
  - "Temporary software glitch in the control system"
  - "Unstable or low household voltage"
  - "Faulty main PCB or inverter PCB"
  - "Loose wiring harness between control boards"
  - "Moisture intrusion affecting connectors"
stepfixes:
  - "Turn the washer off and wait 1–2 minutes before restarting."

  - "Check for moisture around the control panel."

  - "If the error persists, the control boards may require service."
