code: "F33"
meaningshort: "Pump drive system error."
meaninglong: "The F33 error indicates that the washer’s control board cannot properly drive or monitor the drain pump. Whirlpool Cabrio, HE wand Duet-Front load washers use a dedicated pump circuit that monitors current draw and pump speed. If the pump fails to start, draws too much current, or does not respond to control signals, the washer triggers F33. This error is often caused by pump motor failure, wiring issues, or a failing control board."
symptoms:
  - "Washer does not drain"

  - "Cycle stalls before spin"
causes:
  - "Failed drain pump motor"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Blocked pump impeller"
  - "Control board pump driver failure"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the pump wiring harness."
  - "Check the pump impeller for obstructions."
  - "Test the pump motor for proper resistance."
  - "Replace the pump if it does not run."
  - "Replace the control board if pump tests good but F33 persists."
