meaninglong: "The F83 error indicates that the washer’s water level calibration routine has failed. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE top‑load washers periodically calibrate their water level system to ensure accurate sensing. If the pressure sensor does not detect expected changes during calibration, or if the control board receives inconsistent readings, the washer triggers F83. This error is common when the pressure hose is partially blocked, the sensor is failing, or the tub contains residual water during calibration."
