meaninglong: "The F1 E1 error indicates a failure of the washer’s main control board (MCU/CCU). This board manages all washer functions, including motor control, water level sensing, heating, and cycle logic. When the board detects an internal fault, corrupted memory, or voltage irregularity, it triggers F1 E1 to prevent unsafe operation. This is one of the most serious Whirlpool error codes and often requires board replacement."
