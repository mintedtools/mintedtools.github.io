meaningshort: "Motor unable to start or run."
meaninglong: "The F7 error indicates that the washer’s motor could not start or run correctly. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers use a direct‑drive motor controlled by the motor control unit (MCU). If the motor fails to rotate when voltage is applied, or if the MCU detects abnormal current draw, the washer triggers F7 to prevent damage. This error is common when the motor capacitor fails, the motor windings are damaged, or the drive system is obstructed."

  - "F7 appears during wash or spin"

  - "Faulty motor windings"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Obstruction in the drive system"
  - "Failed motor control unit"
