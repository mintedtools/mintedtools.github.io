meaninglong: "The F0 E5 error indicates that the washer has detected an off‑balance condition severe enough to stop the cycle. Whirlpool front‑load washers monitor drum acceleration and vibration levels during spin. If the load cannot be redistributed automatically, the washer halts to prevent damage to the suspension system, bearings, or motor. This code is common when washing single heavy items, mixed‑weight loads, or when the washer is not level."
