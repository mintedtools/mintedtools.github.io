meaninglong: "The F36 error indicates that the washer’s heater relay on the central control unit (CCU) is not functioning correctly. The heater relay controls power to the heating element during hot and sanitize cycles. If the relay is stuck open, stuck closed, or fails to energize, the washer triggers F36. This error is often caused by relay wear, board damage, or overheating."
