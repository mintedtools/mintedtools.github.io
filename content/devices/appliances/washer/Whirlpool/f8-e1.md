meaningshort: "No water detected entering the washer."
meaninglong: "The F8 E1 error indicates that the washer did not detect water entering the drum within the expected time. Whirlpool front‑load washers monitor water flow using a combination of inlet valve feedback and pressure sensor readings. If the washer fills too slowly, does not fill at all, or if the pressure sensor does not detect rising water levels, the washer halts the cycle and displays F8 E1. This error is commonly associated with low water pressure, clogged inlet screens, or faulty inlet valves."

  - "Washer does not fill"

  - "F8 E1 appears before agitation begins"

  - "Low household water pressure"
  - "Clogged inlet valve screens"
  - "Faulty hot or cold inlet valve"
  - "Kinked or blocked inlet hoses"
  - "Pressure sensor not detecting water level changes"

  - "Ensure both water supply valves are fully open."
