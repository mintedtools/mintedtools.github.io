meaninglong: "The F40 error indicates that the washer’s pressure system is not providing valid water level information. Whirlpool Duet washers use a pressure switch or analog pressure sensor to monitor water level. If the sensor output is missing, out of range, or inconsistent with the washer’s fill stage, the washer triggers F40. This error is less common but typically indicates a failing pressure sensor or wiring issue."
