meaningshort: "Low water flow or no water entering the washer."
meaninglong: "The LO FL error indicates that the washer is not receiving enough water within the expected fill time. Whirlpool washers monitor water flow using inlet valve feedback and pressure sensor readings. If the washer detects slow filling, no filling, or inconsistent pressure changes, it triggers LO FL to prevent overheating of the pump and to avoid running a cycle without sufficient water. This code is functionally equivalent to F8 E1 on many Whirlpool front‑load models, but LO FL is displayed on certain user interface versions and specific WFW platforms."
symptoms:
  - "Washer does not fill"
  - "Washer fills very slowly"
  - "Cycle stops shortly after starting"
  - "LO FL appears before agitation begins"
causes:
  - "Low household water pressure"
  - "Clogged inlet valve screens"
  - "Kinked or blocked inlet hoses"
  - "Faulty hot or cold inlet valve"
  - "Pressure sensor not detecting water level changes"

  - "Ensure both water supply valves are fully open."

  - "Inspect the pressure hose for blockages or leaks."
