meaninglong: "The F24 error indicates that the washer’s water temperature sensor (NTC thermistor) is reading values outside the expected range. Whirlpool Duet washers rely on this sensor to regulate water temperature during wash cycles. If the sensor is open, shorted, or providing erratic readings, the washer triggers F24 to prevent incorrect temperature control."

  - "Washer stops early in the cycle"

  - "Washer refuses to start certain cycles"
causes:
  - "Faulty NTC temperature sensor"
  - "Loose or corroded wiring harness"
  - "Moisture intrusion in the sensor area"
  - "Control board misreading sensor values"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the temperature sensor wiring."
  - "Test the sensor with a multimeter (typically 10–12kΩ at room temperature)."
  - "Replace the sensor if readings are out of range."
  - "Inspect the control board if the sensor tests good."
