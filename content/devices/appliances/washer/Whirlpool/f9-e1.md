meaninglong: "The F9 E1 error indicates that the washer took too long to drain water from the drum. Whirlpool front‑load washers monitor drain time using the pressure sensor and pump feedback. If the washer cannot reduce the water level within the expected time, it triggers F9 E1 to prevent flooding and cycle failure. This error is extremely common and typically relates to drain pump blockages, clogged filters, or drain hose issues."
