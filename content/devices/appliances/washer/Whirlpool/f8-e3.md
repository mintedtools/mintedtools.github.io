meaninglong: "The F8 E3 error indicates that the washer has detected an overflow condition. Whirlpool front‑load washers use a pressure sensor to monitor water level. If the water level rises beyond the safe threshold, or if the pressure sensor detects rapid increases inconsistent with normal filling, the washer triggers F8 E3 and activates the drain pump. This error is often caused by a stuck inlet valve, faulty pressure sensor, or siphoning conditions."

  - "Drain hose inserted too far (siphoning)"
