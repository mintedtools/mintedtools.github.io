meaningshort: "Pressure switch failure detected."
meaninglong: "The F32 error indicates that the washer’s pressure switch is not responding correctly. On Whirlpool Cabrio and HE top‑load washers, the pressure switch monitors water level using an air hose connected to the tub. If the switch does not open or close at the correct pressure, or if the hose is blocked or leaking, the washer cannot determine water level and triggers F32. This error is common when detergent residue clogs the pressure hose or when the switch diaphragm fails."
symptoms:
  - "Washer overfills or underfills"

  - "Unexpected draining during fill"
causes:
  - "Blocked or kinked pressure hose"
  - "Loose hose connection"
  - "Faulty pressure switch"
  - "Air dome clogged with residue"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the pressure hose for kinks or blockages."
  - "Ensure the hose is firmly connected at both ends."
  - "Clean the air dome on the tub."
  - "Replace the pressure switch if readings are inconsistent."
