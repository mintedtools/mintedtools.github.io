meaningshort: "Water level out of expected range."
meaninglong: "The F34 error indicates that the washer has detected a water level that is outside the expected range for the current cycle. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers use a pressure switch or analog pressure sensor to monitor water level. If the sensor detects a level that is too high, too low, or inconsistent with the washer’s fill stage, the washer triggers F34 to prevent overfilling or dry operation."
symptoms:
  - "Washer overfills or underfills"
  - "Washer drains unexpectedly"
  - "Cycle stops during sensing or fill"
  - "F34 appears early in the cycle"
causes:
  - "Faulty pressure switch or sensor"
  - "Blocked pressure hose"
  - "Air leaks in the pressure system"
  - "Control board misinterpreting sensor values"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the pressure hose for blockages or leaks."

  - "Replace the sensor if readings are out of range."
