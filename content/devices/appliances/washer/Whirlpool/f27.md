meaninglong: "The F27 error indicates that the washer’s pressure switch is stuck in the 'full' position or is reporting a water level that is too high. Whirlpool Duet washers use a pressure switch to monitor water level. If the switch does not reset after draining, or if the control board detects an abnormally high water level, the washer triggers F27. This error is often caused by a blocked pressure hose, a stuck pressure switch, or siphoning conditions."

  - "Drain hose inserted too far (siphoning)"
