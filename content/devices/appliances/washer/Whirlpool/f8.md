meaningshort: "Water supply or fill error."
meaninglong: "The F8 error indicates that the washer is not receiving adequate water flow during the fill cycle. On Whirlpool Cabrio and HE top‑load washers, the control board monitors inlet valve operation and water level changes. If the washer fills too slowly, does not fill at all, or detects inconsistent pressure changes, it triggers F8 to prevent running a cycle without sufficient water."
symptoms:
  - "Washer does not fill"
  - "Washer fills very slowly"
  - "Cycle stops early"
  - "F8 appears before agitation begins"
causes:
  - "Low household water pressure"
  - "Clogged inlet valve screens"
  - "Kinked or blocked inlet hoses"
  - "Faulty inlet valves"
  - "Pressure sensor not detecting water level changes"
stepfixes:
  - "Ensure both water supply valves are fully open."
  - "Clean inlet screens on the valves."
  - "Check hoses for kinks or blockages."
  - "Test inlet valves for proper resistance."
  - "Inspect the pressure hose for blockages."
