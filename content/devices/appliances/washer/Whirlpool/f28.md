meaninglong: "The F28 error indicates that the washer’s central control unit (CCU) and motor control unit (MCU) are not communicating correctly. Whirlpool Duet washers use a digital serial communication link between these boards. If the signal is interrupted due to wiring issues, board failure, or electrical noise, the washer triggers F28. This error is similar to F11 but typically appears during high‑load motor operations."
