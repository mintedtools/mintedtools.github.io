meaninglong: "The F15 error indicates that the washer’s motor control unit (MCU) has overheated. Whirlpool Duet washers monitor MCU temperature indirectly through current draw and internal thermal protection circuits. If the MCU overheats due to excessive motor load, restricted airflow, or internal component failure, the washer triggers F15 to prevent permanent damage. This error often appears during long cycles, heavy loads, or repeated spin attempts."
