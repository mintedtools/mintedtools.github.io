meaninglong: "The F43 error indicates that the washer’s motor has stalled during operation. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers and Duet front-load washers monitor motor rotation using tachometer feedback. If the motor fails to rotate when voltage is applied, or if rotation stops unexpectedly, the washer triggers F43 to prevent overheating and damage. This error is common when the drive system is obstructed, the motor capacitor fails, or the motor windings are damaged."

  - "Failed motor capacitor"

  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
