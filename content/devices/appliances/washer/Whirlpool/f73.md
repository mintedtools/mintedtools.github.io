meaningshort: "Basket disengagement failure."
meaninglong: "The F73 error indicates that the washer’s basket did not disengage properly when shifting from spin mode back to wash mode. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers rely on the shift actuator to move the drive system between modes. If the actuator cannot fully disengage the basket, or if the drive hub is worn, the washer triggers F73. This error is often paired with F72 and typically points to mechanical wear in the drive system."
symptoms:
  - "Washer will not agitate"
  - "Grinding or clicking noises"
  - "F73 appears during mode transitions"
  - "Washer stalls during spin-to-wash shift"
causes:
  - "Failed shift actuator"
  - "Worn or stripped drive hub"
  - "Binding basket or drive shaft"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the shift actuator for proper operation."

  - "Ensure the basket moves freely."
