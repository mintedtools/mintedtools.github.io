meaningshort: "Pressure switch failure detected."
meaninglong: "The F31 error indicates that the washer’s pressure switch is not responding correctly. Whirlpool Duet washers use a pressure switch to monitor water level during fill, wash, and drain cycles. If the switch does not open or close at the correct pressure, or if the control board receives inconsistent readings, the washer triggers F31. This error is common when the pressure hose is blocked, the switch diaphragm fails, or wiring becomes loose."
symptoms:
  - "Washer overfills or underfills"
  - "Washer stops during sensing"
  - "F31 appears early in the cycle"
  - "Unexpected draining during fill"
causes:
  - "Blocked or kinked pressure hose"
  - "Loose hose connection"
  - "Faulty pressure switch"
  - "Air dome clogged with residue"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the pressure hose for kinks or blockages."
  - "Ensure the hose is firmly connected at both ends."
  - "Clean the air dome on the tub."
  - "Replace the pressure switch if readings are inconsistent."
