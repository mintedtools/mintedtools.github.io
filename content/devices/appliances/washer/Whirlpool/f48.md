meaningshort: "Water temperature out of expected range."
meaninglong: "The F48 error indicates that the washer has detected a water temperature that is outside the expected range for the selected cycle. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers monitor water temperature using an NTC thermistor. If the temperature is too high, too low, or changes too rapidly, the washer triggers F48 to prevent fabric damage and incorrect cycle performance. This error is common when the temperature sensor is failing or when hot and cold water supplies are reversed."

  - "Washer refuses to start certain cycles"

  - "Faulty temperature sensor"
  - "Reversed hot and cold water hoses"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Control board misreading sensor values"

  - "Verify that hot and cold hoses are connected correctly."
