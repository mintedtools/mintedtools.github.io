code: "F52"
meaningshort: "Motor overload detected."
meaninglong: "The F52 error indicates that the washer’s motor is experiencing an overload condition. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers monitor motor torque and current draw through the motor control unit (MCU). If the motor encounters excessive resistance, such as from a jammed drive system or seized bearings, the MCU detects an overload and triggers F52 to prevent overheating. This error is common when the basket cannot rotate freely or when the motor is beginning to fail."
symptoms:
  - "Washer hums but does not move"
  - "Drum struggles to agitate or spin"
  - "F52 appears during wash or spin"
  - "Washer stops abruptly"
causes:
  - "Drive system obstruction"
  - "Seized or worn bearings"
  - "Damaged motor windings"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Overloaded basket"
stepfixes:
  - "Inspect the drive hub and basket for obstructions."
  - "Check motor windings for proper resistance."
  - "Test the motor capacitor (if equipped)."
  - "Replace the motor if it fails electrical tests."
  - "Ensure the washer is not overloaded."
