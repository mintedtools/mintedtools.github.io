meaninglong: "The F0 E1 error appears when the washer detects clothing or other items inside the drum during the Clean Washer cycle. Whirlpool’s control logic expects the drum to be completely empty during this maintenance cycle so it can run high‑temperature rinses and internal cleaning routines. If weight is detected by the motor control or load sensor, the cycle is halted to prevent damage, oversudsing, or ineffective cleaning. This code is common on WFW front‑load platforms and indicates that the washer’s load‑sensing system is functioning correctly."
