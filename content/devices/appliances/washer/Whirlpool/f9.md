meaningshort: "Washer is taking too long to drain."
meaninglong: "The F9 error indicates that the washer did not drain water within the expected time. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE top‑load washers monitor drain time using the pressure sensor and drain pump feedback. If the washer cannot reduce the water level fast enough, it triggers F9 to prevent flooding and cycle failure. This is one of the most common Cabrio errors and is typically caused by drain pump blockages, clogged hoses, or a failing pump motor."

  - "Weak or failing drain pump"
