meaninglong: "The F25 error indicates that the washer’s drive motor thermal protector has tripped due to overheating. Whirlpool Duet washers include a thermal cutoff inside the motor to prevent damage from excessive heat. If the motor overheats due to overload, restricted rotation, or internal failure, the thermal protector opens and the washer triggers F25."
