code: "F0 E2"
meaningshort: "Oversuds condition detected."
meaninglong: "The F0 E2 error indicates that the washer has detected excessive suds inside the drum. Whirlpool front‑load washers use a pressure sensor and motor feedback to determine when foam levels are too high for proper rinsing. Oversudsing can prevent the washer from spinning, cause long drain times, and reduce cleaning performance. This code is common when using non‑HE detergent, too much detergent, or when residue builds up inside the tub."

  - "Washer stops mid‑cycle with F0 E2 displayed"

  - "Washer drains repeatedly without progressing"
  - "Cycle takes significantly longer than normal"
causes:
  - "Using non‑HE detergent"
  - "Using too much detergent"
  - "Detergent residue buildup in the tub"
  - "Blocked pressure hose causing false suds detection"
  - "Drain pump struggling due to foam"
stepfixes:
  - "Run a Rinse & Spin cycle to clear excess suds."
  - "Use only HE‑rated detergent and reduce the amount used."
  - "Run a Clean Washer cycle with affresh or similar cleaner."
  - "Inspect the drain pump filter for clogs."
  - "If the error persists, check the pressure hose for blockages."
safetynotes:
