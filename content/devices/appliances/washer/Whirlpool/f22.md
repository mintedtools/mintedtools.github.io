meaningshort: "Door lock failure."
meaninglong: "The F22 error indicates that the washer’s door lock mechanism failed to engage properly. Whirlpool Duet washers require the door to be securely locked before the cycle can begin. If the control board does not receive confirmation from the lock assembly, or if the lock cannot fully engage, the washer triggers F22. This is one of the most common Duet error codes and is frequently caused by worn latches, misaligned doors, or failing lock assemblies."

  - "Washer will not start"

  - "Door appears closed but not fully latched"

  - "Faulty door lock assembly"
  - "Obstructed latch or gasket"
  - "Loose wiring harness"
  - "Door hinge misalignment"
