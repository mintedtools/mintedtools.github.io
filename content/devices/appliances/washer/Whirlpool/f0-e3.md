meaninglong: "The F0 E3 error appears when the washer detects that the load is too large or too heavy for the selected cycle. Whirlpool’s load‑sensing algorithm monitors motor torque, drum acceleration, and water displacement to determine whether the washer can safely operate. Overloading can prevent proper tumbling, cause excessive strain on the motor, and lead to poor cleaning results. This code is common on WFW front‑load models when bulky items are washed together or when the drum is packed too tightly."
