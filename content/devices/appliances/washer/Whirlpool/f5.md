meaninglong: "The F5 error indicates that the washer’s lid switch or lid lock mechanism is not functioning correctly. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers require the lid to be securely locked before agitation or spin can begin. If the control board does not detect proper lid lock engagement, or if the lid switch is stuck, damaged, or misaligned, the washer displays F5 and halts the cycle."

  - "F5 appears when pressing Start"

  - "Faulty lid lock assembly"
  - "Broken or misaligned lid strike"
  - "Loose wiring harness"
  - "Control board not energizing the lock"
