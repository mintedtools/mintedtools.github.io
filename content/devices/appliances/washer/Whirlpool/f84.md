meaninglong: "The F84 error indicates that the washer’s water level sensor is providing readings outside the expected range. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers use an analog pressure sensor to monitor water level. If the sensor output is too high, too low, or erratic, the washer cannot determine the correct water level and triggers F84. This error is often caused by a failing pressure sensor, a blocked pressure hose, or electrical interference affecting sensor output."

  - "Faulty analog pressure sensor"
  - "Blocked or kinked pressure hose"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Control board misreading sensor output"

  - "Inspect the pressure hose for blockages or leaks."
