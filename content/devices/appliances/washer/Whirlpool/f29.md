meaninglong: "The F29 error indicates that the washer attempted to unlock the door but did not receive confirmation from the door lock mechanism. Whirlpool Duet washers use a solenoid‑driven lock that must disengage before the door can open. If the lock remains engaged due to mechanical failure, wiring issues, or internal binding, the washer triggers F29 and prevents the door from opening to avoid damage."
