meaninglong: "The F14 error indicates that the washer’s central control unit (CCU) has detected corrupted or unreadable EEPROM memory. The EEPROM stores cycle parameters, configuration data, and operational logic. If the CCU cannot read or validate this data, the washer triggers F14 and halts operation. This error is often caused by firmware corruption, voltage spikes, or internal CCU failure."
