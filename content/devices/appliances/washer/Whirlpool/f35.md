meaningshort: "Analog pressure sensor failure."
meaninglong: "The F35 error indicates that the washer’s analog pressure sensor is not providing valid readings. Unlike simple on/off pressure switches, analog sensors provide continuous water level data to the control board. If the sensor output is out of range, erratic, or missing, the washer triggers F35 to prevent overfilling or incorrect cycle operation. This error is common on Cabrio models with digital water‑level sensing."

  - "Control board misreading sensor output"
