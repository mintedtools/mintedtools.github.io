meaningshort: "Motor overheating detected."
meaninglong: "The F54 error indicates that the washer’s motor has overheated during operation. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers monitor motor temperature indirectly through current draw and thermal protection circuits. If the motor overheats due to excessive load, restricted rotation, or internal failure, the washer triggers F54 to prevent permanent damage. This error often appears after long cycles, heavy loads, or repeated spin attempts."
