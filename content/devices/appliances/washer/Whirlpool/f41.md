meaningshort: "Motor control failure detected."
meaninglong: "The F41 error indicates that the washer’s motor control unit (MCU) is not responding correctly. Whirlpool Cabrio and HE washers and Duet front-load washers use a dedicated MCU to regulate motor speed, torque, and direction. If the MCU fails to initialize, loses communication with the main control board, or detects internal faults, the washer triggers F41. This error is often associated with failing MCUs, wiring issues, or voltage irregularities."

  - "Washer becomes unresponsive"

  - "Failed motor control unit"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Voltage spikes or unstable power"
  - "Internal MCU component failure"
stepfixes:
  - "Unplug the washer for 5 minutes to reset the MCU."
  - "Inspect the wiring harness between the MCU and main control board."
  - "Check for signs of overheating on the MCU."
  - "Replace the MCU if communication cannot be restored."
