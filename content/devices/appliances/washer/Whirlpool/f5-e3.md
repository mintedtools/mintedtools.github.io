meaninglong: "The F5 E3 error indicates that the washer attempted to unlock the door but did not receive confirmation from the door lock mechanism. Whirlpool washers use a solenoid‑driven lock that must disengage before the door can open. If the lock remains engaged due to mechanical failure, wiring issues, or internal binding, the washer will display F5 E3 and prevent the door from opening to avoid damage or forced entry."
