meaninglong: "The F1 error indicates that the washer’s main control board (also called the machine control or central control unit) has detected an internal fault. On Whirlpool Cabrio and HE top‑load washers, the control board manages motor operation, water level sensing, lid lock control, and cycle logic. When the board detects corrupted memory, failed relays, or voltage irregularities, it triggers F1 to prevent unsafe operation. This is one of the most serious Cabrio error codes and often requires control board replacement."
