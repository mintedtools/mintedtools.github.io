meaninglong: "The F30 error indicates that the washer’s dispenser system is not functioning correctly. Whirlpool Duet washers use a motorized dispenser mechanism to route water through detergent, bleach, and softener compartments. If the dispenser motor stalls, the position sensor fails, or the dispenser drawer is obstructed, the washer triggers F30. This error is common when detergent residue builds up in the dispenser tracks."
