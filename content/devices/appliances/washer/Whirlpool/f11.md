meaninglong: "The F11 error indicates that the washer’s central control unit (CCU) and motor control unit (MCU) are not communicating correctly. Whirlpool Duet washers use a digital serial communication link between these boards. If the signal is interrupted due to wiring issues, board failure, or electrical noise, the washer triggers F11. This is one of the most common Duet error codes and often relates to loose connectors or failing control boards."
