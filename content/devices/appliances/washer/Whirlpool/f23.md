meaningshort: "Heater failure detected."
meaninglong: "The F23 error indicates that the washer’s heating element or temperature regulation system is not functioning correctly. Whirlpool Duet washers use a heater to maintain water temperature during certain cycles. If the control board detects that the heater is not drawing current, is shorted, or is not raising water temperature as expected, the washer triggers F23. This error is common when the heater burns out, wiring becomes loose, or the temperature sensor fails."
symptoms:
  - "Washer stops mid‑cycle"
  - "Water does not heat during sanitize or hot cycles"
  - "F23 appears during wash"
  - "Cycle takes longer than normal"

  - "Failed heating element"
  - "Loose or damaged wiring harness"
  - "Faulty temperature sensor"
  - "Control board heater relay failure"

  - "Inspect wiring harnesses for heat damage."
  - "Test the temperature sensor with a multimeter."
