meaningshort: "Door lock error detected."
meaninglong: "The F5 E2 error indicates that the washer attempted to lock the door but was unable to confirm a secure latch. Whirlpool front‑load washers use a multi‑stage door lock mechanism that engages electrically and mechanically. If the lock does not fully engage, the washer will not begin the cycle to prevent water leakage and unsafe operation. This error is common when the door latch is obstructed, the lock assembly is worn, or the wiring harness is loose."

  - "Washer will not start"

  - "F5 E2 appears immediately after pressing Start"
  - "Door appears closed but not fully latched"
causes:
  - "Obstructed door latch"
  - "Faulty door lock assembly"
  - "Loose wiring harness to the lock"
  - "Door hinge misalignment"
  - "Control board failing to energize the lock"

  - "Check for clothing or debris in the door gasket."
